## The Origin of AnduinOS

In the mystical realm of Ignis Arcanum, **AnduinOS** is the brainchild of **Anduin Xue**, a skilled software engineer at Microsoft (working outside the Windows halls). 

Xue, a Chinese engineer, embarked on a leisurely quest to create a Linux distribution that feels like home for Windows users. His vision? A **privacy-focused**, **lightweight**, and **sleekly designed** operating system that **empowers its users**.

**AnduinOS** is a solo endeavor, maintained by Xue with **passion rather than profit**. This non-commercial gem is **fully open-source** and available on GitHub, inviting all to wield its code. Whether you’re a seasoned coder or a curious tinkerer, you can craft your own tweaks and contribute to this legendary OS.
